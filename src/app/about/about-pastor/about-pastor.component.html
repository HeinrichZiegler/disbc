The pastor and his better half

Rev. Michael Nicholson

BRS, MDiv, Pastor

Born and raised in Brantford ON, Michael is the youngest of 3 children. The greatest struggle Michael faced was a battle with testicular cancer in 2005 (not the reason he's bald, that's just good ol' genetics at work!). By the grace of God, through the prayers of many friends and family, and through many months of chemotherapy, Michael is now cancer free and has recently had his last ever visit with his oncologist.

Michael started at Dutton and Iona Station Baptist Churches in March 2010. He and his wife Rachel, moved to Dutton shortly after getting married in May 2010. Michael is a passionate preacher, calling the people of God to follow in his ways, not merely observing rules and rituals, but living their whole lives in conjunction with God’s ways.

Michael is a graduate of Tyndale University and McMaster Divinity College. Michael enjoys reading. You’ll often catch him reading the Puritans, along with other modern day writers. He is always on the lookout for new books to add to his ever-growing personal library. He is a self professed 'book geek.' Michael loves to read in order to share what he’s learning with other Christians in order to help them learn and grow in their faith and dependence on God.

When he’s not reading or doing other church related activities, you can find Michael at the local golf course, where he enjoys the company of fellow golfers while trying to improve his fledgling golf game. During the winter months (when he can’t golf) Michael enjoys spending time in his small home workshop where he enjoys cabinet making.

Next to his passion for preaching the Word of God and seeing people grow in their knowledge and love for God, and besides his love for his wife, Michael’s third great love is THE Ohio State Buckeyes football team. Every Saturday from September to November, Michael can be seen (and heard) cheering on his beloved Buckeyes.

Michael and Rachel have added a new member to their family. On Friday June 20, 2014, through adoption, Veronica Elizabeth became to newest member of the Nicholson household.
