About us

A two point charge is made up of two, autonomous churches, joining together to share in the ministry of Jesus Christ. Dutton and Iona Station Baptist Churches have agreed to remain completely autonomous, meaning they make their own decisions, they each have their own church building, style of worship, constitution and church government. They do, however, share a pastor.

A two-point charge presents many unique ministry opportunities. It offers a close relationship with another body of Christ, it allows us to have a far greater reach in impacting our communities with the Good News of Jesus. 2013 marks a milestone in the relationship between these two churches as it will mark the 50th Anniversary of their partnership.
