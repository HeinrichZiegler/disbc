Welcome

Dutton and Iona Station Baptist Churches are two churches in the Municipality of Dutton/Dunwich that partner together in serving Christ and making his name known to our communities.

Our Calling

Dutton and Iona Station Baptist Churches exist to bring glory to God, to make known the name of Jesus, and to make committed, life-long followers of Jesus.
