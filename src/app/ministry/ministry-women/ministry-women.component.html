Womens's Bible Study

Our Women's Bible Study meets every Tuesday night at various locations for a time of fellowship, prayer, and Bible Study. These meetings have been known to go well into the night, as the women enjoy each other's company while discussing the Word of God.

With women coming from both Dutton and Iona Station, it's a wonderful time of getting to know fellow Christian women in the area, building each other up in the faith, and learning with and from each other from God's World.

The Ladies' of Faith group at Iona Station Baptist Church plans and organizes many events and ministries throughout the year.
