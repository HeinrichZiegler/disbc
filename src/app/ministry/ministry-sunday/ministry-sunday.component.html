Sunday School

Children’s Sunday School is a place for our rambunctious children to learn about God’s love through Bible stories, songs, crafts, and games presented by a team of kind, fun loving, and godly teachers. A Nursery is also provide for children up to the age of 3.

While they do have fun, our children are also taught to care for the poor and less fortunate. Our Sunday School kids have raised money to buy chickens, rabbits, and water through various relief organizations.

The Ladies' of Faith group at Iona Station Baptist Church plans and organizes many events and ministries throughout the year.
