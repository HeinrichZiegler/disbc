Men's Bible Study

Our Men's Group meets every Thursday night at various locations for prayer and Bible Study. It's a wonderful time to come together as men, to be open and honest with our struggles, to pray for one another, and to learn from God's word.

With men coming from both Dutton and Iona Station, it's a time of encouragement (with the odd jab), camaraderie with fellow Christian men, and a time to learn with each other and from each other's life experiences.

Our Men's Group also meets once a month for Breakfast, where the wonderful smell of bacon greets you upon arrival! Also once a year, the men's group takes their breakfast out to the local golf course where they enjoy 9 holes following breakfast.
